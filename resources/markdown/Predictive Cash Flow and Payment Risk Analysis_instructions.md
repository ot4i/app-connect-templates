This template analyzes customer payment risk by retrieving sales order data from SAP S/4HANA and using Google Gemini AI to calculate credit standing scores based on transaction patterns and payment terms.

To refer to these instructions while editing the flow, open [the Github page](https://github.com/ot4i/app-connect-templates/blob/main/resources/markdown/Predictive%20Cash%20Flow%20and%20Payment%20Risk%20Analysis_instructions.md) (opens in a new window).

1. Click **Use this template** to start using the template.
2. Click the **SAP S/4HANA** nodes, and if you're not already connected, connect to your [SAP S/4HANA account](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/app-connect/saas?topic=apps-sap-s4hana).
3. Click the **Google Gemini** node, and if you're not already connected, connect to your [Google Gemini account](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/app-connect/saas?topic=apps-google-gemini).
4. To start the flow, in the banner, click **Start flow**.

Use this template to perform predictive cash flow and payment risk analysis by analyzing customer sales orders and updating credit worthiness scores in SAP S/4HANA.
