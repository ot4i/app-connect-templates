## Using the template

1. Click **Use this template** to start using the template.
1. Connect to [Splunk](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/app-connect/cd?topic=apps-splunk).
1. On the splunk nodes select the Application and User.
1. Click on the **Gmail** node, and if you're not already connected, connect to your [Gmail account](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/app-connect/saas?topic=apps-gmail).
1. On the Gmail "Send email" node, provide your __email__ in __To__ field
1. Click **Done**.
1. To start the flow, in the banner click **Start flow**.

Use this template to receive emails for error or exception events that occur in Splunk
