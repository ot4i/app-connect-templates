This template gets a contact data from salesforce and adds to sapsuccessfactor as a candidate and generates a narrative about the candidate with his contact details and sends it to email and sharepoint. 

To refer to these instructions while editing the flow, open [the Github page](https://github.com/ot4i/app-connect-templates/blob/main/resources/markdown/Aggregated%20Salesforce%20and%20sapsuccessfactor%20data%20is%20used%20to%20generate%20narrative%20and%20sends%20to%20Sharepoint%20and%20email%20.md) (opens in a new window).

1. Click **Use this template** to start using the template.
2. Click the **Microsoft Azure OpenAI** nodes, and if you're not already connected, connect to your [Microsoft Azure OpenAI account](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/app-connect/saas?topic=apps-azure-openai).
3. Click the **Salesforce** node, and if you're not already connected, connect to your [Salesforce account](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/app-connect/saas?topic=apps-salesforce).
4. Click the **SAP SuccessFactors** node, and if you're not already connected, connect to your [Sapsuccessfactor account](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/app-connect/saas?topic=apps-sap-successfactors).
5. Click the **Microsoft SharePoint** node, and if you're not already connected, connect to your [Microsoft Sharepoint account](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/app-connect/saas?topic=apps-microsoft-sharepoint).
6. Click the **Gmail** node, and if you're not already connected, connect to your [Gmail account](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/app-connect/saas?topic=apps-gmail).
7. To start the flow, in the banner, click **Start flow**.

Use this template to automate the process of executing summaries aligned with enterprise recruitment data.
