This template automatically creates business partner records in SAP S/4HANA when new leads are created or updated in Zoho CRM, streamlining the supplier onboarding process.

To refer to these instructions while editing the flow, open [the Github page](https://github.com/ot4i/app-connect-templates/blob/main/resources/markdown/Supplier%20Onboarding%20Sync%20from%20ZohoCRM%20to%20SAP%20Vendors_instructions.md) (opens in a new window).

1. Click **Use this template** to start using the template.
2. Click the **Zoho CRM** node, and if you're not already connected, connect to your [Zoho CRM account](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/app-connect/saas?topic=apps-zoho-crm).
3. Click the **SAP S/4HANA** node, and if you're not already connected, connect to your [SAP S/4HANA account](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/app-connect/saas?topic=apps-sap-s4hana).
4. Click the **Gmail** node, and if you're not already connected, connect to your [Gmail account](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/app-connect/saas?topic=apps-gmail).
5. To start the flow, in the banner, click **Start flow**.

Use this template to sync supplier onboarding data from Zoho CRM leads to SAP S/4HANA business partner records.
